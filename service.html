Shipping Policy

We offer fast and reliable shipping to all our customers. Here's how it works:

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹5,000.
  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days.
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (additional charges apply).
  • Tracking: You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order ships.

Return Policy

Not satisfied with your purchase? No problem. Our return policy is simple:

  • 30-Day Returns: Return any unworn frames within 30 days for a full refund.
  • Free Returns: We'll provide a prepaid shipping label for your return.
  • Exchanges: Easily exchange your frames for a different style or color.
  • Prescription Lenses: Custom lenses are non-refundable but can be remade if there's an error.

Warranty Policy

We stand behind the quality of every frame we craft. Your vision is protected:

  • 1-Year Warranty: Covers all manufacturing defects from the date of purchase.
  • Authenticity: 100% guaranteed original Visionix products with certification.
  • Free Adjustments: Complimentary frame tightening and cleaning at any partner store.
  • Anti-Scratch: 6-month protection plan included for all premium lens coatings.

Common Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express delivery reaches you in 2-3 days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day "no questions asked" return policy on all non-prescription frames.

Do you offer prescription lenses?

Yes! We offer a wide range of premium prescription lenses, including single-vision, progressive, and blue-light filtering options. Simply upload your prescription during checkout or email it to our specialists.

How do I find the right frame size for my face?

Every product page includes a detailed "Size Guide." You can also use our Virtual Try-On tool or check the inner temple of your current glasses for three numbers (e.g., 52-18-140) to find your perfect match.

What materials are Visionix frames made of?

We use aerospace-grade titanium, premium Italian acetate, and eco-conscious recycled materials. Every frame is engineered to be lightweight, durable, and hypoallergenic.

What is the Visionix Warranty?

All our frames come with a **1-Year Limited Warranty** that covers manufacturing defects. We also provide a lifetime of free ultrasonic cleaning and minor adjustments at any of our partner boutiques.

Can I change my order after it's been placed?

Since we begin custom-crafting your lenses immediately, changes can only be made within **2 hours** of placing your order. Please contact our 24/7 Priority Support for urgent modifications.
